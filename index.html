About CIC

Citizens' Investment Club was started in October 2019 by a group of members from the class 2012 of Citizens' Secondary School. The founding members came up with the idea after realizing that coming together means growing together, thus a catchphrase "Coming together, as we grow together". The club is open for people of all age groups and professions as long as the interested party is a citizen of Uganda. CIC is registered and regulated by the Investment Act in the Constitution of Uganda.

Our core values are:

  • Working Together
  • Truthfulness
  • Investment
  • Charity
  • Education
  • Accountability

Vision

To have a financial institution that will set the pace in banking and investment.

Mission

Working together as one people in pulling big investment vehicles and doing charitable work to improve the livelihoods of vulnerable citizens.

For the bigger projects, we invest together
